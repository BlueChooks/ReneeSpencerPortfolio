The Give App

Give is designed to encourage regular giving to charity. The user selects any number from a list of partnered charities, links their bank account to Give's system, and whenever they spend money, their payment is rounded up to the nearest dollar and the difference is distributed amongst their chosen pool of charities.
We wanted to design an easy, unobtrusive way to donate regularly. Give breaks down the barriers of commitment and financial strain, with each donation so small as to be unnoticeable, but adding up over time into a tangible positive impact.
This project was a group effort. I was responsible for the wireframes, some of the higher-fidelity pages, and the interactivity of the prototype.
